# Experience Live Music Like Never Before with FanConnect

**FanConnect by gento0** turns your phone into part of a live music light show. Synchronize your flashlight with hundreds of other fans for immersive effects — instantly and automatically.

## Features

• Synchronized flashlights at live music shows and events  
• Instant connection to ongoing performances  
• Fully automatic — no setup required  
• Works on **iOS and Android devices**  
• Hundreds of phones working as one unified light effect  
• More effects and features coming in future updates  

## How It Works

1. Download **FanConnect** on your **iOS or Android** device.  
2. Open the app during a live music show.  
3. Lift your phone and watch your flashlight join the collective light experience.
