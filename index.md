# Experience Live Music Like Never Before with FanConnect

**FanConnect by gento0** turns your phone into part of a live concert light show. Synchronize your flashlight with hundreds of other fans for immersive effects — instantly and automatically.

## Features

- Synchronized flashlights at concerts and events
- Instant connection to live shows
- Fully automatic — no setup required
- Hundreds of phones working as one unified effect
- More effects and features coming in future updates

## How It Works

1. Download **FanConnect** on your iOS device.
2. Open the app during a live show.
3. Lift your phone and watch your flashlight join the collective light experience.
