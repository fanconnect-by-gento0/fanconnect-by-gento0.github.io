# Privacy Policy for FanConnect

_Last updated: December 2025_

FanConnect by gento0 ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how the FanConnect mobile application ("the App") handles information when you use it.

This policy is intended to meet the requirements of **Google Play** and the **Apple App Store**.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

FanConnect does **not collect, store, or share any personal data**.

The App does not require user accounts, registration, or login.

Specifically, we do **not** collect:
- Names
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Contacts or media files

---

## 2. Permissions Used

FanConnect only requests the minimum permissions required for core functionality:

- **Flashlight / Camera (Flash)** — used exclusively to control the device flashlight during live music shows  
- **Internet access** — used to synchronize light effects in real time  

These permissions are used only while the App is active and are **not** used for tracking or analytics.

---

## 3. How the App Works

FanConnect synchronizes flashlight behavior across multiple devices during live music shows. No personal or identifying data is transmitted or stored.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

FanConnect does **not** share any data with third parties.

The App does not use:
- Advertising networks
- Analytics services
- Tracking SDKs

---

## 5. Data Retention

Because FanConnect does not collect personal data, **no data is stored or retained**.

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy

FanConnect does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected, the App is suitable for general audiences.

---

## 7. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to ensure the App operates securely and reliably.
