# FanConnect by gento0 — Support Page

Welcome to the official support page for **FanConnect by gento0**, the app that synchronizes light effects with live music performances for an immersive fan experience.

## About the App

**FanConnect by gento0** is designed to enhance live music shows by syncing your device's flashlight with the music. The app is safe and lightweight: it only requires access to the flashlight and internet.

**Latest Update Highlights:**  
• Synchronizes light effects with music in real-time  
• Works reliably on **iOS and Android devices**  
• Tested extensively to ensure stable and secure performance

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q:** My flashlight isn’t syncing with the music.  
**A:** Ensure your internet connection is stable and that the app has permission to access your flashlight.

**Q:** The app crashes or freezes.  
**A:** Try restarting the app and your device. If the issue persists, contact support via email.

**Q:** Does the app collect personal data?  
**A:** No. The app only accesses the flashlight and internet for synchronization purposes.

## Important Notes

• The app works best during live music shows and may not function properly in offline or simulated environments  
• Make sure your device is running a supported iOS or Android version and has sufficient battery
